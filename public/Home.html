Outfit DoesFit is a solution to online sizing difficulty. With a 5 step simple process, you will be ready to shop online and never worry about wrong sizing again.

About us

Started in 2018, three guys came together and decided that they were done with always finding the wrong size when shopping online. So they came up with the idea for a website that compares their sizes with the different sizes of each store. Integreated with the brands like Nike, Adidas and Gap, they have continued to grow.
